Ordering & Accounts

Do I need an account to place an order?
Creating an account ensures we can share tracking updates, CoA files, and service alerts. Guests may submit a one-time order, but follow-up requests require verification, so we recommend registering beforehand.
What payment methods are accepted?
We currently accept major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire payments for institutional buyers. For ACH or wire, contact billing@vitasynlabs.com for remittance instructions before submitting your purchase order.
How can I check the status of my order?
Sign in to your VitaSyn Labs account and open the Orders tab. Each shipment displays a live carrier link, estimated delivery window, and any compliance holds requiring your attention.

Shipping & Compliance

How quickly are orders dispatched?
Orders confirmed before 2 PM Pacific ship the same business day. Orders requiring custom aliquoting or freeze-dried preparation ship within two business days. We will email you if additional documentation is needed.
What packaging is used for temperature-sensitive peptides?
We pack refrigerated and frozen items in insulated shippers with phase-change packs validated to hold temperature for 48 hours. Temperature indicators are included inside each shipment for quick verification upon receipt.
Can VitaSyn Labs ship internationally?
At this time we limit distribution to the United States. We continue to evaluate additional markets and post updates on our newsletter when expansion is available.

Product Integrity

Do you provide certificates of analysis?
Yes. Each batch ships with a digital CoA that includes purity, solvent content, and analytical method details. You can also request past certificates by emailing lab@vitasynlabs.com with the lot number and order ID.
How should I store peptides after delivery?
Refer to our dedicated Peptide Storage and Peptide Reconstruction guides. In general, store lyophilized vials at -20 °C and reconstituted solutions at 2-8 °C unless otherwise specified.
Can I request additional product documentation?
Absolutely. Submit your request through the Contact page and include the product name, lot number, and the document type needed (stability, SDS, storage logs). Our team will respond within one business day.
